## Quick Start Guide

Get logging in under 2 minutes:

1. **[Invite Wish Logger](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1308500301791105044&permissions=8&scope=bot)** to your server
2. **[Open the dashboard](https://wishlogger.xyz/dashboard)** and select your server
3. **Choose your logging channels** — or let us create them automatically
4. **You're done!** Logs start flowing immediately

Need help? Our [setup guide](/docs/getting-started) walks through everything step-by-step.
